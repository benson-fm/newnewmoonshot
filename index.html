Mission and Goals

ALTA is a start-up that strives to revolutionize how humanity travels in space for generations to come as we continue to tap the waters of the cosmos.

Mission

ALTA was created to create the next generation of space propulsion using Thermal Anti-Matter propulsion to reach the distant red planet. We're going to be accelerating speed and increasing efficiency to reach the Martian surface.

Vision

We aim to get our technology in usage for the purpose of getting humanity our closest celestial bodies, the Moon and Mars, by the year 2034, licensing our technology to space agencies around the world and later expanding.

Why?

Humanity has always been stuck on our tiny floating rock we call home for the majority of our existence with only stepping foot on the moon, we see there's massive potential in the red planet that will be in the best interests in humanity, and we strive to lead the way there.

Thermal Anti-Matter Propulsion

Making the impossible, possible.

Here at ALTA, we love thinking not moonshot or marsshot but starshot. We're combining all of these emerging technologies into one to create a new propulsion system the world has never seen before. Anti-matter propulsion has been in the talks but is difficult to create because of the interactions between matter and anti-matter. Once they come into contact they both annihilate each other and taking that anti-matter up to space is difficult, so, why don't we make the antimatter up in space instead? We're producing anti-matter up in space because there is barely any matter up there letting us able to produce anti-matter, this will be done through conentrating a powerful laser onto a gold foil where positrons (the antimatter of electrons) are formed and are controlled and guided by our electromagnetic system where they will collide in a reactor to produce heat which will heat up our hydrogen propellant providing thrust. Our propulsion system is 8x faster than any modern propulsion systems today helping humanity shorten space travel by a significant amount.
